ABOUT
TREKKING
IN
INDIA

India is considered is a hub for mountain trekking as it offers plenty of trekking opportunities in its diverse geographical regions. The vast Himalayan ranges in long stretch while its huge landscape is ideal for best trekking tours in India. Every diverse regions of India guarantee unique experience. If trekking in the Himalayas of Uttarakhand offers you picturesque views of valleys, Sikkim Himalayas promises panoramic views of Mount Kanchenjunga while a trek to Ladakh Himalayas brings you face to face with bizarre beauty of this region. Visiting thousands years old monasteries enlightens you while roaming on the Zanskar River is thrilling. Planning a trek across Garhwal Himalayas is amalgamation of spirituality and adventure. In some part of the India is ready to teach you traditional culture and lifestyle of people.

Trekking in India is very famous throughout the world. The trekkers from remotest corner of the world come to take life time experience of Indian Trekking Treks. North Indian trekking destination is somewhat similar to paradise for trekkers. The famous trekking destinations of India are Ladakh, Himachal, Sikkim and Garhwal. All these are in Himalayan belt. Himalayas are taken as the best trekking destination in all over the world.
